How to buy

Create a wallet

Download phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.app

Get some SOL

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to $SULLA. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Raydium

Visit raydium.io To Connect Your Wallet. Then Paste The $SULLA Contract Address Into The field To Indicate Which Coin You Wish To Exchange Your SOL for.

Swap SOL For $SULLA

Choose the Amount You Wish to Trade, Click "swap," And Confirm The Transaction. When Your Wallet Prompts You, Confirm the Action. Congratulations, You Are Now A Proud Holder Of $SULLA.
